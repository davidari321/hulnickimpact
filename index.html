About Jill Hulnick

Jill Hulnick, Founder and Principal Consultant of Hulnick Impact Consulting, is a dynamic leader with extensive experience in nonprofit leadership, strategic planning, governance, community engagement, and organizational development. She partners with mission-driven organizations to drive measurable impact and sustainable growth.

Jill brings over two decades of senior leadership experience, including serving in C-Suite and Board of Trustee roles. She is deeply committed to strengthening organizations through strategic planning, leadership coaching, and facilitating effective governance practices. Jill's work is rooted in fostering collaborative, inclusive environments where both people and missions can thrive.

Her expertise spans strategic facilitation, program evaluation, organizational assessments, board development, community-building initiatives, and leadership transitions. Jill has successfully worked with nonprofits, foundations, and civic organizations to align operations with vision, clarify priorities, and maximize resources for impact.

Known for her thoughtful approach, collaborative style, and actionable results, Jill believes that meaningful change happens when vision is transformed into clear, measurable action steps.
